# 10-Day Vietnam Itinerary: Hanoi • Ha Long Bay • Sapa • Da Nang • Ho Chi Minh City

**Travel dates:** Flexible (ensure you are on a Ha Long Bay cruise on the **25th** to catch festivities)  
**Interests:** Sleeper train with a spa/massage experience, scenic highlights, culture, and great food.

---

## Summary at a Glance
| Day | Destination | Plan |
|-----|-------------|------|
| 1 | Hanoi | Arrive, Old Quarter & Hoàn Kiếm Lake |

## Day-by-Day Details

### Day 1 – Arrive in **Hanoi**
- Land at Nội Bài International Airport (HAN). Transfer to hotel in the Old Quarter.
- Evening walk around **Hoàn Kiếm Lake**, **Ngọc Sơn Temple**, and the vibrant street-food alleys.
- Optional: **Water Puppet Show**.

## Practical Notes & Tips
- **25th on the Bay:** Make sure your cruise start date is **24–25th** or **25–26th** depending on whether you want the 25th sunset/night on board.  
- **Trains:** For Sapa, book **Hanoi ↔ Lào Cai** sleeper berths (4-berth soft sleeper recommended). Pair the **Vietage** luxury experience with your **Đà Nẵng ↔ Quy Nhơn** day if you want a true spa carriage—this can be added as an optional detour.  
- **Transfers:** Private shuttles are fastest for Hanoi ↔ Ha Long.  
- **Weather:** Northern regions (Hanoi/Ha Long/Sapa) can be cooler and misty in some seasons; Central & South are warmer.  
- **Cash & eSIM:** ATMs are plentiful; consider an eSIM for data.  
- **Etiquette:** Dress modestly at temples; small cash for tips is appreciated.
